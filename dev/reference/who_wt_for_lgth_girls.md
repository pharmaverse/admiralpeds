# WHO Weight-for-length for girls

WHO Weight-for-length charts for girls from 45cm to 110cm

## Usage

``` r
who_wt_for_lgth_girls
```

## Format

A data frame with 651 rows and 4 variables:

- `Length`:

  Length in cm

- `L`:

  Box-Cox transformation for normality

- `M`:

  Median

- `S`:

  Coefficient of variation

## Source

<https://www.who.int/tools/child-growth-standards/standards/weight-for-length-height>
